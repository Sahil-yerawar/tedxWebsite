About

What is TEDx?

At TEDxIITHyderabad, we believe it is important to invest in areas where the local community finds its relevance. Consequently, TEDxIITHyderabad is an effort to bring together and showcase the bright minds from the local scene- we are continuously in awe of how much Hyderabad has to offer in terms of the quality of ideas and number of people ready to share their stories with the world.As an educational institution, we want to leverage ideas, technologies, design and education to help create a better future, which is is what TED stands for.
